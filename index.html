Scenes

There are three scenes in this narrative visualization. The first author driven scene shows the daily cases count on the timeline and actions taken by New York state. The rest of two scenes focused on daily hospitalized count and death count respectively and they are user driven.

Annotations

All events and actions taken by New York states are annotated on the chart and appeared during animation.

Parameters

There are two parameters in the narrative visualization. The first is type of statistics: case count, hospitalization count of deaths count. The second is time interval. The first scene is using the case count parameter and entire timeline available in the data. The second and third scenes are using hospitalization count and death count respectively with enitre timeline by default.

Triggers

There are three triggers available: The dropdown menu to select the metric. The timeline brush to select the time interval. The tooltip with additional information when user hover cursor over the bar on the chart.
